WELCOME TO

By Team T-reX

GrapeVine is the perfect home for gardeners everywhere, whether you are trying to help grow the next generation of gardeners, or just sprouting, you’ll find your community on the GrapeVine.

Track your gardens and plants to see the fruits of your labour bloom, or get inspired by the incredible gardens shared from all around the world - including your next project!

Engage with your community by making posts: get some advice on how to grow the biggest watermelons, show off a beautiful sunset over your backyard, or even share a selfie during your gardening mahi on a hot summer afternoon, and bond with other horticulturalists in the comments!

If that’s not enough sate your appetite, weed through the assortment of live streams or even create your own to share your tips and tricks, or share the satisfaction of whipping up a delicious salad from scratch. If you’re not gardening yourself, join a stream and chat with other green-thumbed fans to share your thoughts or ask questions to the creator.

Whether you want to show off your sprawling orchard, or get advice on how to keep your first ever potted plant flourishing, you’ll find it on the GrapeVine.
